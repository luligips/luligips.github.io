Powerful Design

Our contemporary metal and mesh ceiling range unites durability with cutting-edge creativity. Select from an incredible variety of colours, shapes, and perforations in classic lay-in systems or special custom-made solutions.

Durability Made of high-quality materials, our robust metal and mesh ceiling solutions provide long-term durability, making them ideal for high traffic areas.

Metal Ceiling Solutions

Choose from our diverse range of metal and mesh ceiling materials to match the aesthetic and architectural requirements of your project.

Metal

Metal ceiling tiles range from classic lay-in and clip-in panels to modular systems in unique sizes, shapes, textures, and colours.

Mesh


Mesh ceilings are available in a variety of patterns and colours for a striking industrial look, and modern alternative to open plenums.

Metal Creative


Our artistic triangular tiles seamlessly combine with square tiles to form parallelograms that blend and balance interiors.

Metal Open Ceilings


Metal canopies and baffles offer endless ways to add drama, colour, and dimension to your design.

Metal Grille


Open cell metal tiles mask the plenum, and elegantly cover lighting, ventilation, and sprinklers while ensuring access to these vital systems.

Performance Features

Our metal ceilings offer important quality benefits, like fire resistance, acoustic comfort, impact resistance, and easy access with swing-down functionality. We also provide specially engineered solutions for more demanding applications, such as seismic performance, security resistance, and chilled system integration, as well as metal tiles with a unique BioGuard® coating for hospitals and clean rooms.
