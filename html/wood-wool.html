Wood Wool Ceilings

Optimal Aesthetics and Acoustics

Made from high-quality materials that balance acoustics and evoke elegance, HERADESIGN® wood wool ceilings offer the perfect combination of style, acoustic comfort, and sustainability. The elegant, natural character of the wood wool surface enables endless possibilities in shape, texture, and colour, making these ceilings suitable for various interiors, from education to entertainment, offices to recreational facilities.

Sustainability Benefits
Circularity HERADESIGN® Fine, Superfine, Micro, and Plano in white and natural colours are accredited Cradle to Cradle Certified® Gold.
Resource Conservation The entire HERADESIGN® range is made from sustainably sourced raw materials obtained through PEFC- and/or FSC-certified forestry.
Recyclability HERADESIGN® products are made from naturally occurring materials (wood, water, and magnesite) and are 100% recyclable, as certified through Environmental Product Declarations.
Durability Due to their high mechanical strength and impact resistance, HERADESIGN® wood wool ceilings offer enhanced durability to ensure a long lifespan.

Performance Features

Materials affect the air we breathe. The non-toxicity of HERADESIGN® wood wool ceilings not only protects building occupants, but also everyone involved in the products’ lifecycle, from manufacturing and installation to recycling. Other performance benefits include high mechanical strength, low shrinkage, and a long lifespan.
