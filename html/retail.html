Ceilings for Retail Buildings

High Quality That Never Goes Out of Style

Ceiling design plays a prominent role in retail venues. It must perfectly complement your concept with the aim of creating an inviting ambience that enhances the shopping experience and streamlines the purchasing process. Whether you’re planning a unique boutique or spacious mall, we offer a wealth of ceiling materials and styles to realise your vision. Play with light and shade, colour and contour to craft inspiring environments. Our durable quality will ensure that your designs stand the test of time.

Benefits of Our Ceilings for Retail Locations
Bespoke design Interior design of retail venues can range from minimalistic to opulent, retro to futuristic, elegant to casual. Our matchless portfolio of multi-material solutions allows you to create tailor-made ceiling designs to suit any aesthetic.
Comfortable acoustics Balanced acoustic environments enhance occupant comfort, aid speech intelligibility, and support privacy. Our ceiling materials offer high sound absorption for clear communication and optimal sound attenuation to reduce distractions between rooms.
Safe air quality High volatile organic compound (VOC) levels can impact occupant health. Most of our ceiling materials are certified by Eurofins Indoor Air Comfort® and Indoor Air Comfort® Gold to have low or very low VOC emissions.
Shopping Mall Concourses & Galleries

Our resilient metal and wood design ceilings are top choices for high-traffic areas. Add spot acoustic options, like canopy and baffle ceilings, in a variety of shapes and colours to enhance sound absorption and attract shopper attention.

Showrooms

We offer many specialty modular ceilings, floating ceilings, and wall solutions as standard options, which can be combined to create unique designs that express brand personality. Complete the look with canopy and baffle systems for a show-stopping statement.

Food Courts

Elevate design and mitigate noise with acoustic ceilings installed over dining areas. We offer a broad range, from standard mineral to metal, wood wool to wood design ceilings, canopies, and baffles.

Boutiques

Give boutiques a personal touch that reflects the retailer’s image with our specialty ceiling designs. Choose from a range of standard options to create customised styles that are out of the ordinary.

Supermarkets & Department Stores

From checkout lines to dressing rooms, pharmacies to bakeries, our ceiling solutions absorb sound to enhance communication between customers and staff. Thanks to superior durability, they also withstand sag to keep designs looking fresh.
