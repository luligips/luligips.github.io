Mineral Ceilings

Stunning, Serene Interiors

Smooth, white, and bright, our mineral ceilings offer the perfect surface for creating intimate, aesthetic spaces. They absorb sound yet reflect light while dazzling with an endless array of possibilities – from wall-to-wall to open, floating ceiling designs. Our mineral ceilings are strictly monitored by an ISO production process to guarantee quality, safety, and responsible use of resources.

Mineral Ceiling Solutions

Mineral Laminated

This smooth, whiter-for-longer range offers extensive edge, module, and acoustic options for complete design flexibility and sound control.

Mineral Plain


Thanks to their smooth, white finish, our plain mineral ceilings ensure excellent light reflectance and high flanking transmission.

Mineral Sanded


With their fine surface texture, our sanded mineral ceilings provide an optimal balance of light reflectance and acoustic comfort.

Mineral Fissured / Perforated


These fissured and perforated ceilings offer a unique combination of superior sound absorption and flanking transmission for advanced intelligibility.

Seamless Acoustic


Our seamless, durable, whiter-for-longer ceilings offer Class A sound absorption for outstanding acoustic control.

Performance Benefits

Crafted for excellence, our premium mineral ceilings deliver outstanding visual and acoustic comfort, while promoting energy savings and hygiene.

Acoustics High sound absorption, high attenuation, or a perfect balance of both can optimise occupant comfort, intelligibility, and privacy.
Reflectance Excellent light reflectance boosts occupant comfort, reduces dependence on artificial lighting, and helps conserve energy.
Hygiene Easy-to-clean mineral ceilings greatly enhance comfort and facilitate hygiene in sensitive locations (e.g. healthcare facilities).
Made-to-Measure Mineral Ceilings

There’s no limit to creativity with our mineral ceilings. Choose from a variety of ceiling surfaces to meet your specification needs. Or add drama to your architecture with open ceiling designs, like baffles and canopies, which allow effortless installation and relocation. For added creativity, our Vario Design colours offer a stylish palette of eight nature-inspired hues, as well as the freedom to combine different ceiling materials and shapes into distinctive designs.
