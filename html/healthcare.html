Ceilings for Healthcare Buildings

Thoughtful Design That Welcomes Wellbeing

Careful interior design of hospitals is vital. Patient rooms, waiting zones, and operating theatres all have rigorous safety standards. We are well-versed in the complexities of healthcare facilities. Antibacterial, washable, fire-resistant, and low in emissions, our hospital ceilings uphold the highest standards of hygiene and safety. Yet our designs don’t feel sterile. Optimal light reflectance and acoustic balance support patient comfort and staff productivity. Choose from a multitude of shades and shapes to create therapeutic and tranquil environments.

Benefits of Our Ceilings for Healthcare & Food Production
Antimicrobial protection Whether in healthcare or food production, keeping interiors as hygienic as possible is fundamental in preventing the spread of disease. Our hygienic ceiling tiles are cleanable and specially coated to limit the growth of bacteria.
Comfortable acoustics Balanced acoustic environments enhance occupant comfort, aid speech intelligibility, and support privacy. Our ceiling materials offer high sound absorption for clear communication and optimal sound attenuation to reduce distractions between rooms.
High light reflectance Natural light has a positive effect on human mood and productivity. Returning up to 88% of light (versus 75% with standard ceilings), our ceiling tiles enhance occupant wellbeing and reduce the need for artificial lighting, thus also saving energy.
Hospitals

Our mineral and metal ceilings follow the French standard NF S 90-351:2013 – the most stringent hygiene guideline in Europe for healthcare facilities. Thanks to a special antimicrobial coating, our ceilings help prevent the spread of diseases and infections. They also offer high light reflectance and excellent acoustics for the comfort of patients and personnel alike. Combined, these benefits are known to improve the healing process and reduce length of stay.

Clean Rooms

We offer specialised mineral ceiling solutions for cleanrooms producing electronics, semiconductors, micromechanics, optics, biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, or medical devices. Our cleanroom ceilings support strict hygiene standards in compliance with ISO 14644-1.

Care Homes

Create the perfect environment for individual care. Our ceilings’ enhanced acoustics improve communication between elderly and staff, while their high light reflectance brightens up every room and every face. Moreover, their antimicrobial performance greatly simplifies hygiene.

Nurseries

Countless shapes and colours, high light reflectance, optimal sound absorption, plus easy to clean – our ceiling solutions offer everything you need to create a fun, warm atmosphere where young minds will love to learn and play.

Food Processing Industry

To support the strict hygiene and safety standards of the food industry, we offer a special range of commercial ceiling tiles that are coated to limit the growth of bacteria and fungi. Besides production safety, our solutions enhance staff productivity through great acoustics and light reflectance.
